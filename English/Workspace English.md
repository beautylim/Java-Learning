# Workspace English
point: 
* when ask people to do: **be polite**, **soften words**
* when people ask you to do: **clarify the deadline and clarify the requirement**
* when give the feedback to someone's result: **positive** and **polite**, do not say boring, bold, bad this kind of words.

## Sample 
```
- Anna, this is a good point, let's circle back to it.
- Sure, when would you like to revisit it?  (force clarity)

```

```
- Interesting idea. (I don't like it)


- Anna, your proposal is interesting. (I don't like your proposal)
- I am glad you think it's interesting. Do you have any concerns?
```

```
- Can you take a look at this? (Please fix it as soon as possible)
- Sure, is it urgent?
```
